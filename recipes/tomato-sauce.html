Tomato Sauce

...back to index

Simple spicy tomato sauce from scratch for multiple dinners.

Ingredients

  • 6 1/4 pounds tomatoes, crushed
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoons chili seasoning mix
  • 1 teaspoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon minced onion
  • 1 tablespoon dried oregano
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoons finely minced fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoons white sugar

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan over low heat, combine tomatoes, olive oil, pepper, chili seasoning, salt, onion, oregano, garlic powder, parsley and sugar. Simmer 1 hour. Serve.
